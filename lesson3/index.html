Our Purpose

The purpose of the site is to provide information to the public to help support local buisnesses. We also seek to recognize buisnesses for giving back to the comunity. This site will have information on how buisnesses can join the chamber, where citizens can find locally owned buisnesses, and how to contact memberss of the chamber. Domain name www.farmingtoncomerce.com

Chamber of Commerce

A chamber of comerce is a collection of buisnesses in a local area, usually a city or a town. They promote eachother's buisnesses, hold events for the comunity, and share ideas on how to improve eachother's buinsess. A website for a chamber of comerce needs to have historical information about the city, a directory of participating buisnesses, information on joining the chamber, and information about contacting the chamber.
