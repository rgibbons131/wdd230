About Us

We are Bountiful Foods, a localy owned buisness here in beautiful, costal, Southern California. We Specialize in growing and selling local and all organic produce. From kale, to letuce, to berries, to apples, we have it all. With our first location in Carlsbad California, we found that our buisness was growing faster than we could keep up with, so we decided to expand, and one of our locations is near you in Santa Barbara.
